\section{CLI Command Reference (Abbreviated)}
\label{app:cli}

The \texttt{hoox} CLI exposes 25 command groups. Table~\ref{tab:cli} lists commands most relevant to deployment and evaluation.

\begin{table}[h]
  \centering
  \caption{Selected \texttt{hoox} CLI commands.}
  \label{tab:cli}
  \small
  \begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Command} & \textbf{Purpose} \\
    \midrule
    \texttt{hoox onboard} & Full project bootstrap \\
    \texttt{hoox deploy all --auto} & Deploy all Workers and dashboard \\
    \texttt{hoox check health} & Probe Worker health endpoints \\
    \texttt{hoox perf fastpath run --n 50} & Synthetic internal latency measurement \\
    \texttt{hoox perf fastpath tail --duration 60} & Stream live probe latencies \\
    \texttt{hoox monitor kill-switch on/off} & Halt or resume trading \\
    \texttt{hoox infra provision} & Provision D1, KV, R2, Queues, Vectorize \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}
